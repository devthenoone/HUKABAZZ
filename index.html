Our Story

A ritual reimagined for those who notice the details.

Hookah Artist was born from a simple obsession — that the moment a coal first kisses tobacco should feel like a small act of theatre.

We source single-origin leaves from the foothills of Latakia, blend them slowly with cold-pressed glycerine and rare botanicals, and rest them in oak for forty-two days. What lands on your bowl is not flavour — it is memory, vapour, conversation made visible.
